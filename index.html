SRI SIDDHIVINAYAKA Temple is famous temple in Bangalore. The Godness of the temple were Lord Ganesha,Lord Murugan, Lord Bhuvaneshwari, Lord Ayyapa and Lord Hanuman.The temple was build by following Agama Sastras.

Township Temple committee has taken the initiative to renovate the Sri Sidhivinayaka Temple and Pakshala. The Temple requires attention and is in a reamshackle condition.The Temple renovation plan has been drawn up according to the Agama Sastras. We appeal for liberal donations for this noble and divine cause need your unnstinted support to meet the cost of constructtion of this divine edifice.
